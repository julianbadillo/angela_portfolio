Design Process

Understand

Objective

Promote the benefits of cereals, specificaly beans, among cereal farmers market, customer, and public presenting laboratory test information in way that general public could easy navigate across the beans nutricional information.

Client

Fenalce is the National Federation of Cereals (corn, beans, wheat, and legumes ), located in Cota, Cundinamarca. Colombia. The association main aim is increasing cerals consumption and production by promoting research, offering technical solutions, and including best practices.

Define

Problem Statement

Fenalce required to present nutricional information of beans including laboratory test results. This information must be organized and easy to undersatand for general public.

Observation

Technical information sometimes is difficult to understand. Their complexity requires to be familiar with basic concepts; however, the way in which it is presented could increase understanding.
